Flipper Clicker Lib

So much information is being lost, lets store it!

The idea behind this project initially was to store the encoding for the buttons/commands from all of the random remote controls (both IR and RF-based) I have in my house/garage. I figured other folks probably had the same issue, and if they had lost a remote they needed, but had a FlipperZero and cloned the Flipper Clicker Lib project repo,they could regain lost functionality and keep things out of the landifll just a little bit longer. I'll footstomp this through this site: mending is better than ending in so many ways. Anyways, I started using my own FlipperZero to snarf up the transmissions of several devices that I needed to store or thought might be of use, and I hosted them on github.

I haven't updated it in a while, so I might find something interesting to include first...
